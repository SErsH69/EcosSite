How Do I Pay the Hosting Fee?

The hosting fee covers electricity costs, 24/7 ASIC performance monitoring, and data center security. Choose between Prepayment or Postpayment options: Prepayment deducts daily from mining rewards, while Postpayment entails monthly payments at the start of each month.
